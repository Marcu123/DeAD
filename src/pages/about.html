Who are we?

About Us

Welcome to the Detention Admin Administration (DeAD) - a comprehensive web application designed to streamline the visitation process for individuals serving sentences in penitentiaries or correctional facilities. Our mission is to facilitate meaningful connections between inmates and their visitors, ensuring that the process is efficient, secure, and beneficial for all parties involved.

Our Vision

At DeAD, we believe in the power of connection and the positive impact it has on rehabilitation. We understand the importance of maintaining relationships despite challenging circumstances, and our application serves as a bridge between the inside and the outside world.

Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a secure, user-friendly platform that respects the dignity of all users and promotes positive outcomes for inmates and their families. Our team is continuously working to enhance DeAD based on user feedback and the latest technological advancements.

Get in Touch

We value your feedback and suggestions. If you have any questions or would like to learn more about how DeAD can support your facility or organization, please contact us.

Thank you for choosing DeAD - where connections foster rehabilitation and hope.
